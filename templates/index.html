What factors affect the value of a used car?

Several factors, including mileage, make, model, year, and condition, can influence the actual worth of a car. These factors are considered during our price predictions.

How can I determine if the dealer is offering a fair value for my trade-in?

Our model estimates the fair value of your car based on various attributes. Compare this estimate with the dealer's offer to assess fairness.

Our model considers relevant features to provide a recommended price. You can use this estimate as a starting point and adjust based on market trends and other factors.

What data sources do you use for price predictions?

Our predictions are based on machine learning algorithms trained on historical data. While no prediction is perfect, our model aims to provide reliable estimates.
